Augmented Virtuality Prototype V1.0

Hand tracking combined with full color video see-through.

Posted by Jacob H Clarkson on August 25, 2016

Leap + Oculus + Webcam

The system now uses the Leap Motion Controller purely for hand tracking. Rather than the infra-red video feed from the Leap, the viewports at the hands locations are now passed video data straight from a webcam which is mounted on the top of the HMD.
